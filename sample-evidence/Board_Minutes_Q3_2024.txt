AGENDA ITEM 3: BSA/AML PROGRAM UPDATE
============================================

BSA Officer Sarah Johnson presented the quarterly BSA/AML report.

Key Discussion Points:

1. SAR Filing Activity
- 47 SARs filed in Q3 2024
- Consistent with prior quarters
- No significant trends identified

2. Transaction Monitoring Performance
- Alert volumes stable at approximately 1,200/month
- False positive rate: 78% (target: <80%)
- Average investigation time: 2.3 days

3. Transaction Monitoring Tuning Update
- BSA Officer presented the NEW TUNING METHODOLOGY DOCUMENT for Board approval
- Document establishes quarterly review schedule
- Includes threshold adjustment criteria and approval requirements
- Chief Risk Officer confirmed alignment with regulatory expectations

4. Regulatory Updates
- FinCEN proposed rulemaking on beneficial ownership
- Staff monitoring developments and will report impacts

5. Training Status
- Annual BSA training 94% complete
- Deadline extended to November 15 for remaining staff

Board Discussion:
Director Johnson inquired about the tuning methodology approval process. BSA Officer confirmed that all threshold changes require BSA Officer approval, with significant changes escalated to the Board Risk Committee.

Director Brown asked about peer benchmarking. BSA Officer indicated thresholds are compared against industry surveys and vendor recommendations.

RESOLUTION 2024-42: BSA/AML Policy and Tuning Methodology Approval

RESOLVED, that the Board of Directors hereby approves:
1. The updated BSA/AML Policy Version 2.1
2. The new Transaction Monitoring Tuning Methodology document

Motion by: Director Johnson
Seconded by: Director Williams
Vote: Unanimous approval
